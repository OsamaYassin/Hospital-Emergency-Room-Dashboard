# Hospital-Emergency-Room-Dashboard
## 📊 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive, data-driven analysis of an Emergency Room's operations over 19 months (April 2023 - October 2024), tracking over 9,216 unique patient records. The project focuses on measuring operational efficiency, identifying wait-time bottlenecks, understanding patient demographics, and optimizing hospital resource allocation.

## 🚀 Key Insights
* **Wait Time Bottlenecks:** **59.31%** of the patients missed the target wait time threshold, with an overall average wait time of **35.3 minutes**. This indicates a critical need for triage workflow restructuring.
* **High-Volume Demographics:** Young adults aged **20–29** represent the highest volume spike with **2.4K patients**, nearly doubling the volume of any other individual age group.
* **Peak Demand Windows:** Heatmap analysis reveals severe crowding during early mornings on **Mondays and Thursdays (04:00–06:00)** and late-night weekends **(22:00–00:00)**.
* **Non-Emergency Strain:** Over **5.4K patients** had no specialist referral ("None"), and **1.8K** fell under General Practice, showing that the ER is heavily loaded with low-acuity cases that could be diverted.
* **Patient Satisfaction:** The average satisfaction score remains exceptionally strong at **4.99 out of 5**, though slight seasonal drops are visible during the winter months (November to February).

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
1. **Implement Triage Fast-Tracking:** Introduce a rapid-screening mechanism to separate minor cases immediately, aiming to reduce the 35.3-minute average wait time.
2. **Optimize Staff Scheduling via Heatmap:** Dynamically increase healthcare staffing during verified peak hours (Mon/Thu early mornings and weekend nights).
3. **Establish an Adjacent Urgent Care Clinic:** Divert the 5.4K non-referral and 1.8K General Practice patients to a co-located walk-in clinic to offload the main ER team.
4. **Deploy Winter Surge Staffing:** Add temporary capacity and front-desk administrative support from November through February to counter seasonal satisfaction score drops.

## 🛠️ Tools & Tech Stack
* **Power BI Desktop:** Dashboard design, interactive visualizations, and canvas layout.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Calculated measures for average wait times, patient satisfaction trends, and conditional formatting rules.
* **Power Query:** Data cleaning, type transformation, and structural modeling.
